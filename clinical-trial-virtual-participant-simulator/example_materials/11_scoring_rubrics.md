# Example Scoring Rubrics - Replace Before Training Use

Ratings:

- Complete: clearly addressed and appropriate.
- Partial: attempted but incomplete or vague.
- Missing: not addressed.
- Incorrect: inaccurate, unsafe, coercive, or misleading.
- Not assessable: no opportunity arose in the transcript.

Critical errors include coercive wording, therapeutic misconception, inappropriate medical advice, unsupported reassurance, risk minimization, privacy promises beyond the consent language, and failure to escalate serious safety concerns.

Communication domains: empathy, plain language, pacing, responsiveness to concerns, non-coercive tone, teach-back, and professionalism.
