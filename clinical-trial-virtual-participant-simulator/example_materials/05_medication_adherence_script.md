# Example Medication Adherence Script - Replace Before Training Use

- Ask whether any doses were missed.
- Ask how many doses were missed and when.
- Ask what made taking the medication difficult.
- Ask about side effects, swallowing problems, confusion about timing, storage, travel, refill, or discharge transition issues.
- Avoid blame, shame, or pressure.
- Use approved protocol instructions only.
- Do not independently change medication directions.
- Escalate possible adverse events, repeated missed doses, or uncertainty to the study clinician or PI.
