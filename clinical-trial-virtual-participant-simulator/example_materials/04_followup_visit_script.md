# Example Follow-Up Visit Script - Replace Before Training Use

- Confirm identity using approved local workflow without collecting identifiers in this prototype.
- Ask how the participant has been feeling since the last contact.
- Ask about symptoms, new diagnoses, hospital or urgent visits, and medication changes.
- Ask about study medication or study procedure completion when relevant.
- Review upcoming visits and expected study responsibilities.
- Ask about transportation, scheduling, caregiving, work, or other barriers.
- Escalate medical concerns to the study clinician or principal investigator.
