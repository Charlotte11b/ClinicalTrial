# Example Participant Questions FAQ - Replace Before Training Use

Common participant questions:

- Will my doctor know I am in the study?
- Who can see my information?
- Could I get placebo or usual care?
- Will this replace standard treatment?
- What side effects should I watch for?
- Will I be paid or reimbursed?
- Can I leave the study later?
- Will I receive study results?
- What should I do if I miss a dose or miss a visit?

Staff should answer within the approved script and refer medical decisions to the study clinician.
