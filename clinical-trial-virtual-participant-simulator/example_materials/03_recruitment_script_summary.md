# Example Recruitment Script Summary - Replace Before Training Use

Use a respectful, non-coercive approach:

- Introduce yourself and your role.
- Ask if now is a good time to talk.
- Explain that the person may be eligible for a research study.
- Give a brief purpose statement.
- Emphasize that participation is optional.
- Avoid language that implies guaranteed benefit.
- Pause often and invite questions.
- Offer to involve family, caregiver, interpreter, or another support person when appropriate.
