# Example Withdrawal and Retention Guidance - Replace Before Training Use

- Respect participant autonomy.
- Avoid pressure, guilt, exaggerating benefit, or minimizing concerns.
- Clarify whether the participant wants to stop study medication, stop visits, stop data collection, or withdraw completely.
- Ask permission to understand concerns and barriers.
- Offer reasonable, approved options such as rescheduling or connecting with study staff.
- Explain next steps and contact pathways.
- Escalate to study leadership according to the protocol.
