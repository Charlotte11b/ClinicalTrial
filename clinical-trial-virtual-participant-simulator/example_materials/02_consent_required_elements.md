# Example Consent Required Elements - Replace Before Training Use

- Explain why the person is being approached.
- Explain study purpose in plain language.
- Explain voluntary participation and the right to decline.
- Explain that declining will not affect care.
- Explain withdrawal rights and what may happen to already collected data.
- Explain study groups, randomization, placebo, control, or usual care when relevant.
- Explain procedures, visits, medication schedule, expected time, and follow-up.
- Explain foreseeable risks, side effects, discomforts, and privacy risks.
- Explain possible benefits without overpromising.
- Explain alternatives to participation.
- Explain confidentiality and data handling.
- Explain costs, reimbursement, or compensation.
- Provide study team contact information.
- Invite questions and check understanding with teach-back.
